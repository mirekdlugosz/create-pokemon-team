Cookies and tracking mechanisms

We use cookie-like technology to store part of data on your device. Data is used to hide call for action box on main page. It is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you as a person on this or any other website.

We also use Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics to get some idea about popularity of the site. Both may use cookie-like technology to store some data on your device and might identify you across multiple websites. They are subject to Google Privacy Policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics using tool they provide.
